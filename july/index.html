Kaizen Session
July - Interviewing

Photo by Kalen Emsley on Unsplash

“How can we ask the best questions to get the best results?”

Topics & Tools

  • What do we hire for?
    • technical fit
    • cultural fit
    • future potential
    • needs of the current project
  • How do we evaluate our candidates?
  • How do ensure our process is fair, unbiased and consistent?
  • Do we evaluate differently for remote or contractor positions?
  • What do we want candidates to know about what it’s like to work here?
  • How can we accurately sell ourselves and employ self-selection?
  • Are we marketing ourselves appropriately to current employees and prospective employees?
  • Are we asking the questions that matter? How do we know?
  • What are the qualities of our best hires? What attracted them to us?
  • Can we bring more of our company values and goals into the interview process?
  • The "hidden job market", networking vs. job listings
  • Balancing expenses (upfront, long-term, opportunity)
